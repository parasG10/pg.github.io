About Me

Hi! I'm Paras, the guy who fixes what's broken. As a PSM1 Scrum Master, I turned around struggling products and stepped into Product Manager roles when leadership was on leave, consistently getting teams back on track when projects were falling apart.

After proving I could rescue products in tech, I spent 2 years running a small jewelry business where I learned what really matters: managing difficult stakeholders, developing new business relationships, and identifying the bottlenecks that kill productivity. Running your own operation teaches you things from a very unique perspective, and it is an experience I'm truly grateful for.

Now I'm building on that foundation through my MSc in Business Analytics at the University of Nottingham while actively seeking roles in business analysis, product management, product analysis, or scrum leadership where companies need someone who understands both the data and the business reality behind it.

Most people theorize about business problems. I've actually solved them.
